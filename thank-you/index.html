Your Privacy, Our Priority

At Promptly, we take your data privacy seriously. Here’s how we ensure your information stays secure:

🔒 Browser-First Data Storage

Your notes and task lists are stored directly in your browser, ensuring they never leave your device. No shady databases, no third parties—just your data, under your control.

🌐 Cloud-Free by Default

Promptly is designed with a privacy-first approach, meaning your tasks and notes are accessible only to you unless you decide otherwise.

🤖 AI Features with Transparency

When you use our AI-powered features—like task generation, note refinement, or OCR processing—specific snippets of your data are securely transmitted to our servers to enable these capabilities. Rest assured, this data is handled with the utmost care and used solely for the task at hand.

We believe that productivity tools should work for you, not the other way around. That’s why we’re committed to keeping your data safe, secure, and private.
